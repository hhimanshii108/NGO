Home

The Slums to Classroom campaign is a social initiative aimed at providing education and resources to children living in slums. The campaign is focused on improving access to quality education for children living in poverty-stricken areas, where many families struggle to afford basic necessities such as food and shelter, let alone education. This campaign provides supplementary education to the children outside of the classroom through extracurricular activities, such as sports and music programs, and providing health and nutrition support. The ultimate goal of the Slums to Classroom campaign is to break the cycle of poverty by providing children living in slums with the education and resources they need to succeed in life. The campaign is driven by a passion for improving the lives of marginalized children and creating a more equitable society.
