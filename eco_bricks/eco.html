Eco bricks

HOME

Eco bricks are plastic bottles filled with non-biodegradable waste such as plastic bags, candy wrappers, and styrofoam, packed tightly to create a building block. These bricks can be used in construction projects as an alternative to traditional bricks. They are made from materials that would otherwise end up in landfills and are also a sustainable building solution as they reduce the need for traditional bricks, which are often made from clay and require a significant amount of energy to produce. Additionally, eco bricks can also be used in community-based projects such as building schools, houses, and other structures. It is a creative solution to plastic pollution and it makes people more aware of their plastic consumption.
