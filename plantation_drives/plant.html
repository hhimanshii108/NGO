The Green Vibes Only campaign is a movement that promotes environmentally-friendly and sustainable living. It encourages individuals to make small changes in their daily lives to reduce their impact on the environment. These changes can include using reusable bags and water bottles, reducing meat consumption, and conserving energy. The campaign also raises awareness about the importance of protecting the planet and the need for collective action to address climate change. Overall, the goal of the Green Vibes Only campaign is to create a more sustainable future for all. The Green Vibes Only campaign also often organizes plantation drives, as planting trees is an important step in promoting sustainable living and combating climate change.

Through plantation drives, the Green Vibes Only campaign aims to not only raise awareness about the importance of trees but also to actively contribute to their preservation and growth. These drives are typically held in public parks or other community spaces and are open to volunteers of all ages. They also educate people about the importance of maintaining the plantation and how to care for the plants.
